What to Expect

Recruitment for Sigma Eta Pi typically takes place at the start of each semester. We’re looking for passionate students who are excited about entrepreneurship, innovation, and collaboration.

Events include info sessions, tabling, fun social mixers, and professional workshops to get to know our chapter and culture. Our goal is to find driven students eager to grow and contribute to our entrepreneurial community.


Key Dates

  • Info Session: January 16
  • Speed Dating: January 22
  • Applications Due: January 26
  • Interviews: January 30–31
