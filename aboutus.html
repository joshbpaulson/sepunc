Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate the next generation of entrepreneurs by building a strong community of students who are passionate about starting ventures, solving real-world problems, and making a lasting impact.

Our Values

At SEP, we value integrity, creativity, collaboration, and grit. Whether you're pitching a startup, leading a workshop, or volunteering in the community, we hold ourselves to high standards and push one another to grow as innovators and people.
